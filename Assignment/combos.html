Pure P.U.N.K. Combos

1 Card | Combo 1: Bread and Butter Standard Combo

Cards Needed: Emergency Teleport, Ze-Amin, or Ogre Dance

  1. Get to and summon Ze-Amin
  2. Ze-Amin effect: Add Foxy Tune
  3. Foxy Tune effect: Send itself and 1 other card to Special Summon Sharakusai
  4. Sharakusai effect: Fuse into Rising Carp
  5. Rising Carp effect: Tribute itself to summon Deer Note and Wa Gon
  6. Wa Gon effect: Search Jam Session and activate it
  7. Synchro Wa Gon and Deer Note into Jam Dragon Drive
  8. CL1 Dragon Drive to search Madame Spider; CL2 Deer Note to reborn Sharakusai
  9. New chain: Draw 1 card with Jam Session
  10. Jam Session effect: Banish 1 P.U.N.K. in the Graveyard to Special Summon Madame Spider from hand
  11. Madame Spider effect: Search a P.U.N.K. trap
  12. Draw 1 card with Jam Session
  13. Set the trap card you searched with Madame Spider

Result: 2 draws, 1 pop/negate, quick bounce 4 on your opponent's turn, an additional draw on your opponent's turn, and 5000LP on your turn, 4400 on your opponent's turn.

1 Card | Combo 2: Weaker Line but Plays Around Nibiru

Cards Needed: Emergency Teleport, Ze-Amin, or Ogre Dance

  1. Get to and summon Ze-Amin
  2. Ze-Amin effect: Add Foxy Tune
  3. Foxy Tune effect: Send itself and 1 other card to Special Summon Deer Note
  4. Deer Note and Ze-Amin Synchro into Dragon Drive
  5. CL1 Dragon Drive to add a Psychic card; CL2 Deer Note to reborn Ze-Amin; CL3 Ze-Amin to chain block
  6. Synchro into Psychic End Punisher and pass turn

Result: Psychic End Punisher (towers) with 4 cards in hand and 6800 LP. While not an advisable endboard it's better than having just a token on the field.

2 Card Combo: Alternative Route

Cards Needed: Emergency Teleport or Sharakusai and 1 P.U.N.K. in hand

  1. Summon Sharakusai
  2. Sharakusai effect: Fuse into Rising Carp with 1 P.U.N.K. in hand
  3. Rising Carp effect: Tribute itself to summon Deer Note and Wa Gon
  4. Wa Gon effect: Search Jam Session and activate it
  5. Synchro Wa Gon and Deer Note into Jam Dragon Drive
  6. CL1 Dragon Drive to search Madame Spider; CL2 Deer Note to reborn Sharakusai
  7. New chain: Draw 1 card with Jam Session
  8. Jam Session effect: Banish 1 P.U.N.K. in the Graveyard to Special Summon Madame Spider from hand
  9. Madame Spider effect: Search a P.U.N.K. trap
  10. Draw 1 card with Jam Session
  11. Set the trap card you searched with Madame Spider

Result: Same endboard as the 1.5 card combo, but via a different route. Slightly more LP so Psychic End Punisher would be weaker on the crack back.
